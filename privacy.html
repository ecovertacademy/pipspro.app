Privacy Policy PIPS Pro™

Last Updated: March 3, 2026

PIPS Pro™ ("we," "our," or "us") operates pipspro.app and the PIPS Pro™ application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: When you register, we collect your name and email address. Payment information is processed directly by Paddle, our Merchant of Record – we do not store your payment details.

Inspection Data: Property addresses, photos, assessment ratings, and notes you create within the app.

Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our Services
  • To process transactions via Paddle
  • To send you updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • To improve and personalize your experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

Paddle (Merchant of Record): All payments are handled by Paddle.com. We share your email and order details with Paddle to process transactions and provide customer support. Paddle's privacy policy applies to payment data.

AWS S3: Photos you upload are stored on Amazon Web Services servers.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS 1.3 encryption, secure data storage, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting privacy@pipspro.app.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are in the EEA or California, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@pipspro.app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or by posting a notice on our site.
